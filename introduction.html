Personal Background

Wahaj is a data analyst with a bachelors in business administration specializing in Operations management. He embarks his journey by immersing himself in various roles to gain exposure to new ideas, knowledge, and skills, all in pursuit of continuous improvement as a data analyst.

For Wahaj, data is an asset that has the potential to reshape the future for our world. With the right tools, the way data is collected, cleaned, processed, and analyzed can change the way we present and perceive information. As the world perpetually adapts to new technology such as AI, cloud computing, extended reality, and the datafication of the world, Wahaj seeks to yield positive results from this change.

Wahaj believes the method of storytelling is the most important aspect when it comes to data analysis. He strives to be an excellent data storyteller, as he believes it is crucial to frame information to be clear, concise, and memorable for his stakeholders. His areas of expertise equip him with the knowledge and skills to turn the abstract coldness of statistics and data to something relatable for his clients.
